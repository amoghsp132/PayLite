About PayLite

PayLite is a modern payment gateway designed to simplify and secure online transactions for businesses and users worldwide.

Our Journey

Founded in 2025, PayLite emerged from a vision to revolutionize the payment industry. Our team identified the complexities businesses face with traditional payment systems and set out to create a solution that is both user-friendly and secure.

Over the years, we've grown as a small idea to a trusted name in digital payments, serving thousands of businesses across the globe. Our commitment to innovation drives us to continuously enhance our platform to meet the evolving needs of our clients.
